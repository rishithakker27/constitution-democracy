# The 14 Constitutional Dimensions

Ranked by CatBoost feature importance (Polyarchy target). Full variable mappings are in [`data/data_inputs/`](data/data_inputs/).

| Rank | Dimension | Description | Example CCPCNC Variables |
|------|-----------|-------------|--------------------------|
| 1 | Political Competition | Competitive multi-party elections, universal suffrage, rights to form parties | Multi-party system, universal suffrage, right to form parties |
| 2 | Institutional Accountability | Audit institutions, ombudsman, legislative oversight of executive action | Independent audit body, ombudsman, anti-corruption commission |
| 3 | Civil Liberties | Individual freedoms of expression, assembly, religion, and movement | Freedom of speech, assembly, religion, movement |
| 4 | Emergency Powers Constraints | Limits on states of emergency: declaration, duration, rights suspension | Legislative approval required, duration limits, non-derogable rights |
| 5 | Civilian Control of Security | Military and police under civilian authority | Civilian commander-in-chief, legislative approval of deployment |
| 6 | Transparency / Info Access | Right to government information and press freedom protections | Right to information, freedom of press, open government |
| 7 | Rule of Law / Due Process | Fair trial rights, habeas corpus, due process | Right to counsel, presumption of innocence, prohibition of torture |
| 8 | Federalism / Decentralization | Powers distributed between central and sub-national governments | Regional governments, devolution of powers, fiscal federalism |
| 9 | Executive Constraints | Formal limits on executive authority | Term limits, veto override, approval requirements |
| 10 | Amendment Rigidity | Barriers to constitutional modification | Supermajority requirements, referendum, unamendable provisions |
| 11 | Judicial Independence | Judicial autonomy through tenure, appointment, and removal provisions | Security of tenure, independent appointment, jurisdictional protection |
| 12 | Socioeconomic Rights | Positive rights to economic and social goods | Right to education, health, housing, labour rights |
| 13 | Legislative Autonomy | Parliamentary independence from executive control | Legislative immunity, committee powers, budget authority |
| 14 | Equality | Non-discrimination for gender, minorities, and indigenous groups | Gender equality, minority protections, indigenous rights |
